Jaipur

The city of Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and is known for its various attractions like Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, Jala Mahal etc.
It is popular by the name Pink City and is the largest city in the Indian state of Rajasthan.
It is also believed to be one of the initially planned cities of the nation.
Apart from the attractions, there's a lot that can be found in this city that can be explored for a great travel experience.
